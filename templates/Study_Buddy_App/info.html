Information

  • The Home screen serves as the central hub of the application, offering access to key functionalities through navigation tabs.
  • Top Navigation Bar:
    • Contains tabs such as Subject, Schedule, Task, Submission, Reports, and Information, enabling users to switch between different sections.
  • Bottom Navigation Bar:
    • Displays copyright information ("Copyright Study Buddy 2024") and provides a "Back to Top" option for easy navigation within long pages.

  • Users can add and delete subjects using the plus symbol (+).
  • Clicking the plus icon opens a subject form to create a new subject.
  • Mandatory to fill in the subject name and description in the subject form.
  • Press submit to update the list of subjects.
  • After creating a subject, open it by clicking on the subject title tab. You will have two options available: either to create a topic or a task within the subject.

Creating a Topic:

  • Click the plus symbol (+) at the end of the Topic section to open the add topic form.
  • Fill in the Topic Name and submit to update the list of topics.

Creating a Task:

  • Click the plus symbol (+) at the end of the Task section to open the Add Task form.
  • Write the Task name and deadline, then press submit to update the list of tasks.

  • 7-day calendar for planning study sessions.
  • Students can update schedules for the next 7 days.
  • Previous sessions/tasks auto-delete after the 7th day.
  • Choose between creating study sessions for topics or tasks.
  • Notification for schedule overlaps; automatic adjustments if acknowledged.
  • Example: If a session is from 1 pm to 3 pm and a new task is added from 1 pm to 2 pm, the calendar will adjust.

  • Display a list of tasks with their deadlines.
  • Users can mark tasks as completed and submit them.
  • Users have the option to delete tasks.

  • Displays a list of tasks that have been submitted, along with their submission dates.
  • Users can view their submitted tasks and track their progress.

  • Provides a summary of study time hours spent during the week across different subjects and topics.
  • Users can review their study time allocation and progress for better organization and planning.
